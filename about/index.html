Born at Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL)


Our mission at Hackframe (abbr. xF) is to develop an open platform of hardware-software co-designed applications that fosters collaboration, faster product development cycles, and open-source development in the domain of edgeAI and high-performance computing.


We are building a centralized platform designed to streamline the distributed complex problems while efficiently orchestrating and testing crowdsourced solutions. By bringing together problem solvers from diverse backgrounds, our platform fosters innovation and collaboration, ensuring that solutions are developed, refined, and validated in a structured manner. This approach not only enhances efficiency but also democratizes problem-solving, allowing a wide range of contributors to engage in meaningful challenges without the barriers of traditional research and development constraints.

To make it all possible, we are assembling a lean, cracked team of the world’s best engineers passionate about open-source systems and nothing else. If that’s you, we offer an opportunity to do your life’s work and help solve some of the most important technical challenges of our age.

Write to us at hello@hackfra.me to join the team, share your ideas, or just say hello.
